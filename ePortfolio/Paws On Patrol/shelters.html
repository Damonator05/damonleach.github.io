Local Shelters & Partners

Rolla Animal Control and Shelter

A municipal shelter operated by the Rolla Police Department, housing nearly 1,800 animals yearly. They focus on public safety and find loving homes for abandoned pets.

Address: 1915 Sharp Rd, Rolla, MO 65401

Visit Official Website

Phelps County Animal Rescue

A 100% volunteer-run, no-kill shelter located in nearby St. James. They are dedicated to caring for stray and abandoned animals until forever homes are found.

Address: 745 E Springfield St, St. James, MO 65559
